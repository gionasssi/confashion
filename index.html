Fast fashion companies entrust
their production to garment
suppliers around the world.
Some of these companies share their suppliers list. Searching them on Google Maps, it turns out that people uploaded their photos.
More than 6000 pictures were gathered from Mango’s factories’ locations.
New stories from the perspective of these places are told through these photos.
